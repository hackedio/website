A few other things...

Travelling by train?

Most train companies including Eurostar release their cheapest advance tickets between 70-90 days in advance. So check now to grab a bargain.

Planning a hardware hack or something grander?

If you are planning to build anything big or involving more than basic soldering drop an email to help@hacked.io

Under 18?

If you are between the age of 16 and 18, you can attend HACKED. However you can't stay with us overnight so please plan to head home by 8pm.

Attendees under the age of 16 must be accompanied by a parent or other responsible adult and get written approval from the organisers.
