Volunteers

HackDays on the scale of HACKED are not possible without an amazing team. Over the next few weeks we will be posting here roles which we need filling for HACKED.

All the roles are completely voluntary and un-paid however we will look after you and make sure you get regular breaks, meals and have a lot of fun! All volunteers will need to attend a HACKED staff briefing at Telefonica Digital's office in Central London on either July 9th and 11th at 6:30pm.

To volunteer for any role complete the form below.

July 6th Update

All roles are now full, Thanks for your help!

Strategic Hacked Invitation Enrolment and Label Division (S.H.I.E.L.D)

Ready to be the face of HACKED? You will be the first member of the team to greet attendees and be their first port of call during the weekend for any problems. Key responsibilities include:

  • Registering attendees (issuing badges, fixing names etc)
  • Answering attendee questions (issues with internet, how do I get on the roof?)
  • Keeping our front of house experience second to none.

Commitment: Flexible: Friday 10am-9pm, Saturday 8am to Sunday 8pm

The Hacked International Response Supply Team (T.H.I.R.S.T)

Your job is to deliver refreshment at the speed of light! Know your coke from your diet pepsi? Or which chocolate bar perfectly compliments bangers and mash you should signup for THIRST. Responsibilities include:

  • Handing out ice-cold sodas
  • Keeping the sugary supply chain moving
  • Watching out for caffeine overload

Commitment: Flexible during following times: Friday 10am-9pm, Saturday 8am to Sunday 8pm

Hacked Evolved Running and Operations Enlisted Section (H.E.R.O.E.S)

Our support operations team will be the glue that holds together the entire event.Whether it's sneaking beer into the venue, helping to move the giant H cube on to the stage or simply making sure everyone is fed and watered. Your skills will be put to the test in helping make the event run smoothly.

Commitment: Flexible during following times: Saturday 8am to Sunday 8pm

Hacked Editors, Reporters, Announcers and Logistics Department (H.E.R.A.L.D)

While it's not the mid 90's we are in need of a team of web masters to help maintain the HACKED intranet for attendees, take photos, monitor twitter and generally keep information flowing. You will be tested on your knowledge of the blink and marquee tag.

Commitment: Flexible: Friday 10am-9pm, Saturday 8am to Sunday 8pm

The Hacked Unicorn, Network Delivery and Electrical Reserve (T.H.U.N.D.E.R)

Know your DHCP from your DNS? We are looking for people to support our network provider, help attendees with more complex network issues and conduct visual pat testing.

  • Helping with network connectivity issues on Mac, Windows and Linux
  • Maintaining services including RubyGem, PyPi and Brew mirrors, NFS and Web
  • Crimping CAT5

Commitment: Pre-event build plus weekend support

FAQs?

Do volunteers get tickets to HACKED?

Yes, all volunteers will get tickets to HACKED. However most roles have a minimum of approx 10 hour commitment during the weekend so your ability to hack something may be slightly curtailed.

Can you provide hotels, travel etc?

No sorry!

Any other benefits?

As a volunteer you will have all your food and drink covered for the weekend, you will also get regular breaks.
