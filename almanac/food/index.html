Food

Hungry? During the weekend we'll provide drinks, snacks and main meals.

Saturday Lunch

At 13:00 a selection of wraps will be available for all attendees. Vegan/gluten free/lactose free options will be available on request.

Saturday Dinner

From 17:00 'til 19:00 attendees will be able to collect a dinner voucher (and a swag bag) from the reg/help desk. This dinner voucher will be for a specific restaurant and will allow you to choose a set menu meal from the list below. We only have a limited number of vouchers per restaurant (but enough in total for all attendees!), so it will be on a first-come first-served basis.

Thai Silk

  • Sweet & sour chicken
  • Traditional Thai green chicken curry
  • Stir-fried pork with ginger and mushrooms
  • Stir-fried beef with holy basil leaves
  • Stir-fried mixed vegetables (vegetarian)
  • Stir-fried rice with chicken and prawns

All the above come with a soft drink and either steamed fragrant rice or egg noodles.

GBK

  • Small burger (choice of beef; cheese; chicken or veggie)
  • Small side (chunky fries; homeslaw or simple green salad)

Served with a bottle of mineral water or glass of Coke or Sprite.

The O2 Lounge

  • Chilli con carne
  • Sausage and mash
  • Mushroom, tarragon and sherry sauce pasta (vegetarian)

All served with a drink.

Zizzi

  • Rigatoni con pollo e funghi
  • Spaghetti bolognese
  • Spaghetti pomodoro e bufala (v)
  • Risotto di pollo e funghi
  • Pizza fiorentina (v)
  • Pizza quattro stagioni
  • Pizza diavola
  • Insalata di pollo

All items subject to availablity.

Sunday Breakfast

We'll be serving a selection of Danish pastries, croissants and mini muffins from 08:00.

Sunday Lunch

At 12:00 a selection of salads will be available. Please ask if you require Vegan/gluten free/lactose free options.
