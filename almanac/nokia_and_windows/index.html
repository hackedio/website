You’ll be able reach more people on more devices and keep more of your hard earned profits. Simply, you’re closer than you think. Learn more here.

Innovation on Windows Prize

At HACKED, we’re excited to announce a special prize category – Innovation on Windows. This prize will be awarded to an individual or team that has has worked on a new project which exemplifies tough problem solving and maybe even turning their ideas into a business. The projects will be judged on Sunday and the winners announced at the prize giving ceremony. The winner(s) will each receive a Nokia Lumia Windows Phone developer device (worth £400 each). Get your thinking caps on now and we look forward to seeing your idea during the event.

NOKIA MUSIC CHALLENGE

If you’re looking to develop a music related Windows Phone app during HACKED then why not use the Nokia Music API. It will give you access to data on 22 million songs and over 3 million artists. The API’s are a collection of web service and Windows APIs that allow you to offer elements from Nokia's Music service (as available in Nokia Music for Lumia) within the look & feel of your own application.

We are rewarding the team with the best app developed using the Nokia Music API, Nokia Lumia Phones with a full year of Nokia Music+ subscription and Monster earphones. Judging will take place on Sunday by the Nokia Music team.
