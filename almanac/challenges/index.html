Challenges

We will have a few nice challenges for you that may or may not end up with you getting some amazing prizes. All of the following challenges are open to teams of up to 4. Good luck!

General Challenges

Judges' Choice Award

We will have a panel of judges going around to judge your hacks on the Sunday. Don't worry, you don't need to enter if you don't want to, but if you do then you stand the chance to win a very nice prize. The winning team will receive tickets to a gig of their choice at The O2, subject to availability.

Our judging panel currently consists of:

  • Daniel Applequist (Telefonica)
  • Neil Whitworth (O2)
  • John Stevenson (Heroku)
  • Pedro Lamas (Nokia Music)

Public Choice Award

Be your own Judge and vote for your favorite hack on the Sunday. The winning team will receive tickets to a gig of their choice at The O2, subject to availability. We won't list all the judges, this page isn't long enough!
