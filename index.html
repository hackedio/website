What is HACKED?

HACKED is a free-to-attend, intense weekend of learning, building and sharing cool stuff. Whether you are an expert or novice, developer or designer, join us July 20th & 21st at The O2 for what we guarantee will be a memorable experience.

Learn

Throughout the weekend HACKED will feature interesting presentations and kickass workshops by awesome developers.

Build

Hacking is all about playing with technology and using it in new and creative ways. Go on and build stuff!

Share

Don't just attend HACKED. Teach others what you know. Share your knowledge.
