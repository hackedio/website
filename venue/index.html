Venue

Our home for the weekend is the IndigO2 at The O2. Since opening its doors in 2007, it has hosted acts including The Who, Prince and Penn & Teller. Now IndigO2 plays host to you and 499 other developers, designers, hardware hackers and tinkerers. For the weekend we will be taking one of the worlds leading entertainment venues adding a super fast internet line, tables, chairs and of course beanbags to make one awesome HACKED venue.
