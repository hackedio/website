Questions & Answers

What is HACKED?

HACKED is a free-to-attend, intense weekend of learning, sharing and building cool stuff. Whether you are a beginner or an expert, developer or designer, join us for what we guarantee will be a memorable experience.

Hacking is all about playing with technology and using it in new and creative ways. We're not talking about building battle-hardened products - many of the more memorable hacks from similar events have been pulled off thanks to crossed fingers and lots of sticky tape!

If you have ever been to a hack day before, you'll know the fun of making new things quickly, experimenting and collaborating with people from all sorts of backgrounds and levels of experience.

Lots of companies are getting involved in HACKED and they are going to make their APIs and products available. There will be plenty of hands-on sessions and one-to-one help, so don't be scared about getting involved!

Where and when is HACKED?

We will be taking over IndigO2, inside The O2 in London on July 20th and 21st 2013.

Who is running HACKED?

HACKED is being organised by The Lab which is part of O2 with the support of Geeks of London.

Can I give a talk?

Yes! During May and June we will open up our call for proposals for you to submit an idea.

How do I get a ticket?

Tickets to HACKED are now sold-out. We encourage you to join the waiting list for the best chance to still attend HACKED.

I have a great distance and many borders to cross?

If you are planning to come from far away (another country, planet or dimension!) and tickets are not available please drop an email to help@hacked.io. We will do our best to accommodate you!

Can you provide visa letters?

Sadly we are unable to provide any bespoke letters or paperwork for visas. if you're unsure if you need a visa to travel to the United Kingdom you should speak to your local British Consulate or check FCO.gov.uk.

I am under 18 can I attend?

If you are between the age of 16 and 18, you can attend HACKED. However you can't stay with us overnight so please plan to head home by 8pm. Attendees under the age of 16 must be accompanied by a parent or another responsible adult and get written approval from the organisers.

If you are planning to come from far away (another country, planet or dimension!), drop an email to help@hacked.io and we will do our best to help.

What about hardware hacks?

Yes, they are more than welcome at HACKED! However, if you are planning something more than light soldering please get in touch.

Can I stay overnight?

Yes, you are more than welcome to bring your sleeping bag! Alternatively, there are plenty of hotels and guest houses nearby.

Can I volunteer or help out?

Yes, we are currently looking for volunteer "HackMakers" (sort of like the Olympics had GamesMakers— although we can't promise you'll meet the programming equivalent of Tom Daley). If you're interested drop us an email help@hacked.io and include details about your special powers..

Have another question?

We're always happy to answer any questions you have. Drop us an email on help@hacked.io.

HACKED is organised by The Lab with support from Geeks of London
